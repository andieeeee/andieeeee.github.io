About

Hi! I'm Andie. I am a junior at Northwestern University studying Computer Science and Gender & Sexuality Studies. Thank you for visiting my website!


My areas of interest are in front-end design and video game development. I hope to use my gender studies background and personal experiences of disability to create diverse and accessible games!


I'm from Wichita, KS, Jefferson City/Columbia, MO, and am currently residing in Chicago, IL.


In my free time, I love to dance and play Minecraft, Persona5, and the Sims.

I have two cats, Kitty (9) and Rosie (1). They are the most precious beings, as evidenced by the Cat Emergency button on my homepage!


Proudly Latina, LGBTQ+, and disabled.
