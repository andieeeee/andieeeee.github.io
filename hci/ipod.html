Design

All of the iPod mockup is created from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.


Functionality

  • Clicking on the first album will bring you to its song list.
  • Clicking on the fourth song will bring up the song player.
  • The player page has a number of functionalities.
    • Toggle volume up and down
    • Pressing play "begins" the song, pressing pause "stops" the song (Note: No audio will be played, but the time will change.)
    • Can go to previous or next song.
  • The back arrow will take you to the previous page, with the "My Music" page being the start.
